# Privacy Policy

**Reminder2Cal**
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2026

## Overview

Reminder2Cal is a macOS menu bar application that synchronizes your Apple Reminders with your Calendar. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

**The short version:** Reminder2Cal works entirely on your device. It does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Period.

## Data Access

Reminder2Cal requires access to:

- **Reminders:** To read your reminders for synchronization
- **Calendar:** To create and manage calendar events based on your reminders

This access is used solely to perform the core functionality of syncing reminders to your calendar. All operations happen locally on your Mac.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports sent externally
- No advertising identifiers
- No tracking of any kind

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:

- **Configuration:** Stored in `~/Library/Application Support/Reminder2Cal/`
- **Logs:** Stored locally for troubleshooting purposes only

No data is ever transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

## Network Access

Reminder2Cal does not connect to the internet. The app has no network capabilities and operates entirely offline.

## Third-Party Services

Reminder2Cal does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with anyone because we do not collect any data.

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is managed by macOS:

- Your data is protected by your Mac's built-in security features
- The app runs in a sandboxed environment
- Access to Reminders and Calendar requires your explicit permission

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access:** All your data is on your device
- **Deletion:** Uninstalling the app removes all app-related data
- **Revocation:** You can revoke Calendar or Reminders access at any time via System Settings > Privacy & Security

## Children's Privacy

Reminder2Cal does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the app's repository and this document. The "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
